About Me

PhD-qualified Pharmaceutics professional with specialized expertise in the design, characterization, and in-vivo evaluation of nanovesicular drug delivery systems, including the successful development of ThermoSomes for enhanced antifungal therapy.

Currently working as a Regulatory Affairs Executive in the medical devices sector, with hands-on experience in Clinical Evaluation Report (CER) preparation, systematic literature review, and regulatory documentation in compliance with EU MDR and international guidelines.

Possesses industry exposure through collaborative research with Sun Pharmaceutical Industries, involving stability studies and formulation evaluation. Recognized author of multiple peer-reviewed scientific publications and active journal reviewer, demonstrating strong scientific writing and critical appraisal capabilities.

Additionally expanding expertise in healthcare data analytics using Power BI, Python, and SQL to support regulatory intelligence and data-driven decision-making. Passionate about integrating scientific research, regulatory science, and analytics to advance safe and effective healthcare technologies.

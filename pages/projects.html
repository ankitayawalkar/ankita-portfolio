FDA Medical Device Regulatory Intelligence Dashboard

Tools Used: Power BI, Power Query, DAX

Developed an interactive analytics dashboard using FDA 510(k) and PMA datasets (1976–2024) to generate regulatory market intelligence. The project involved data cleaning, dimensional modeling, DAX-based metrics, and visualization to identify innovation trends, competitive positioning, and regulatory growth patterns.

This project demonstrates integration of regulatory domain expertise with modern data analytics tools.
